A note about Camp Moja

Camp Moja is a fee-based program designed to bring joy, connection, and meaningful skill-building to every child who attends. Our team will be in touch soon with details about registration, scheduling, and program fees.

💙 Does your child have an autism diagnosis?
If your child has a diagnosis and your family is interested in ABA therapy services through Moja Behavioral, there may be a pathway for your child to participate in camp as part of their clinical programming — potentially at no out-of-pocket cost to you, depending on your insurance coverage.

To explore this option, we'll first want to connect with you to learn more about your child's needs and determine the right fit. Our team will reach out to schedule a brief conversation.
🌟 Siblings are always welcome!
Brothers and sisters are welcome to join camp on a drop-in basis — a great way for the whole family to be part of the fun. Just let us know when we connect and we'll make sure there's a spot for them.
What happens next
1
We'll reach out to you — a member of our team will contact you using the information you provided.
2
We'll have a conversation — we'll ask a few questions to understand your child and your family's goals, and share everything you need to know about the program.
3
We'll find the right path — whether through direct camp enrollment or a clinical services conversation, we'll make sure your family has clear next steps.

Questions in the meantime? We'd love to hear from you.
