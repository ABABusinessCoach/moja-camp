🎒 Daily Pack List — Everything fits in one bag!
  • Sunscreen — applied before drop-off & packed for reapplication
  • Bug spray — we spend time outdoors every day
  • Lunch + snack — packed from home, nut-aware please
  • Water bottle — labeled with your child's name
  • Change of clothes — a full outfit in a zip-lock bag
  • Water shoes or sandals — for water play activities
  • Closed-toe shoes — worn at drop-off for outdoor activities
  • Any comfort or sensory items — a favorite fidget, stuffed animal, or anything your child absolutely needs to feel at home
🏷️ Label Everything
  • Write your child's first and last name on every item — water bottle, bag, shoes, clothing, and lunch box.
  • Use a permanent marker or iron-on labels — stickers tend to fall off during water play.
  • Lost & found is cleared every Friday — unclaimed items are donated after one week.
💡 Good to Know
  • No electronics — phones, tablets, and handheld games stay home. Comfort devices (e.g., a specific video for transitions) are fine — just let staff know.
  • Medications — must be in the original labeled container with a completed medication authorization form on file.
  • Absences — please call or email as soon as possible, no later than 6:00 AM for same-day absences. All other planned absences must be communicated before the start of camp.
💛
Bring What Makes Your Child Feel Safe
Every child is different — and that's exactly why they're here. If your child has a comfort item, a preferred snack, a specific sensory tool, or anything else that helps them feel settled and ready to have fun, please pack it. Our staff will always follow your child's lead.
Questions? Our team is always happy to help — reach out before camp starts so we can make sure your child's first day is a great one.
